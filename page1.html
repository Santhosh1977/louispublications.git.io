About Louis Publications(R), Mangaluru

It was established in June 2019 as a Partnership Firm with Ms Sunitha Louis & Ms Jacqueline Salins as working partners with the sole objective of simplifying Graduate Education by providing quality text material in the form of Text Books & Educational CD's.
So far we have published 13 titles and 2 CD's. Thanks to our students and teachers patronage, we have published, printed and distributed more than 12000 copies of various text across the state of Karnataka and beyond. We will continue with that journey and also touch upon students of schools as well in the near future, definitely with your support.
